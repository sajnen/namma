Project Overview

Namma Nildana is a community-driven accessibility survey documenting the conditions of bus stops along the Sarjapur Road corridor. Through detailed observation and data collection, we identify existing infrastructure, map missing stops, and assess accessibility features to improve transit equity.

Purpose

Our goal is to create a comprehensive, visual database of bus stop conditions that empowers city planners, transit advocates, and commuters to make informed decisions about urban accessibility. By combining field research with interactive mapping, we make transit infrastructure data accessible to everyone.

Key Objectives

  • Document existing bus stop conditions
  • Identify gaps in transit infrastructure
  • Evaluate accessibility and safety features
  • Support evidence-based urban planning
